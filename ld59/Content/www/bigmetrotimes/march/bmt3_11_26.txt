BIG METRO TIMES

LithOS Vulnerability Could Have Crashed Global Economy
Wednesday, March 11 2026
Fritz Wallace

A vulnerability in the LithOS operating system that would have allowed remote code execution on 8.5 million devices around the world.

The vulnerability, discovered by white hat hacking group Free Movement Society, could have allowed any hacker to infect virtually any machine running LithOS.

A patch was released several weeks before the vulnerability was made public.

Many in the valley have speculated that the new LithOS CEO would have a hard time transitioning from game development to software. The company did not respond to our request for comment.
