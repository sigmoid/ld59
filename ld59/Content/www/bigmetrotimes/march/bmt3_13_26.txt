Mining Accident Kills 2 in Chile
Friday March 13, 2026
Author: Fritz Wallace

2 workers were killed in an explosion at a lithium mine in Chile. 

Anastasia CEO Lance Nightingale had this to say about the tragedy: "We take worker safety extremely seriously. We are devastated by the news out of Chile. Our investigation found that user error caused a blast to go off prematurely. While the mine is closed for repairs, we will be issuing extensive training to all workers in our company."

This follows another similar incident that took place last October in which 3 workers died.
