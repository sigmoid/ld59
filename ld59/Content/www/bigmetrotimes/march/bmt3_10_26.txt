Anastasia Eyes Acquisition Deal With Scram! Games
Tuesday March 10, 2026
Fritz Wallace

Rumours of The Anastasia Corporation moving into the games space have been floating around the valley for months now. Today, the rumours were confirmed.

Scram! Games CEO Rose Cantrell announced that they have opened negotiations with Anastasia for a full buyout.

Scram! has been a juggernaut of the games industry since the inception of the company in 1983.

Since losing their founder and original CEO Ed Sled 4 years ago, Scram! has faced a downturn. Despite releasing 10 games in that time, new business practices have damaged the companies reputation with gamers.
