New Software Announcement Shakes Up Social Media Space
Saturday March 14, 2026
Author: Fritz Wallace

Eutropia, a non profit software company, announced the coming launch of their new product, Zaira, later this year. Zaira is the first social media platform to fully encrypt user data.

This announcement comes at a time where leading social platforms are embattled in controversy over user the practice of selling user data.

Critics claim that, without central access to user data, Eutropia will not be able to effectively moderate the content posted on their platform.
