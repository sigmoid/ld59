Mining Disaster In Chile
Friday October 10, 2025
Author: Fritz Wallace

A mine in Chile operated by The Anastasia Corporation suffered a tragic accident today. 3 workers are missing after a cave in. The cave in was likely triggered by an earthquake that was reported in the area.

*** Update: After our coverage was released, it was revealed that the accident was an explosion and was unrelated to the earthquake. The workers are confirmed dead.
