Eutropia CEO Rose Cantrell Murdered in New York Highrise
3/20/26
Author: Fritz Wallace

Rose Cantrell, CEO of Eutropia LLC was found dead in her penthouse apartment in lower Manhattan this morning. In a brief press conference outside the crime scene, police chief Frances Ballard only said "We are opening a homicide investigation at this time". Cantrell was a prominent business leader and member of many influential New York social circles.
