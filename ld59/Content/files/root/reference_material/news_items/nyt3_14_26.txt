New Software Announcement Shakes Up Social Media Space
3/14/26
Author: Fritz Wallace

Eutropia LLC announced the coming launch of their new product, Zaira, later this year. Zaira is a social media application which a Eutropia spokesperson claims achieves a 14.7 rating on the Weisman Addiction Scale.

This announcement threatens the dominance of The Anastasia Corporation in the social space. Their own flagship product on achieves a 9.3 on the Weisman scale, if the claims from Eutropia are true, Anastasia could be in trouble.

Most Anastasia employees aren't responding for comment, however we spoke to one Tier III executive who candidly admits that The Anastasia Corporation had exhausted every resource in trying to improve their Weisman score. It is doubtful that they will be able to compete when Zaira hits the market in Q4 of this year.
