Welcome to The Anastasia Corporation! As a new employee, we expect you to learn our company values, and retain a copy of them to remind yourself what we stand for.

1. Make the world a better place by any means neccessary
At the anastasia corporation we represent the bleeding edge, where innovation meets institutional power. It is our mission to make the world a better place. Some criticize our methods for accomplishing this end. We ask those critics why they are opposed to a better world. 

2. Respect the bottom line
At the end of the day, we are a business. And businesses have to make tough choices from time to time. When this happens, we are comforted in knowing that our employees are smart enough to understand that anything senior management does, it does for a reason. 

3. Love eachother
We insist that every member of our organization feel love for eachother. Of course, this does not permit office relationships, but platonic love between coworkers is not just desired, it is expected.

Glory, Glory, Anastasia
